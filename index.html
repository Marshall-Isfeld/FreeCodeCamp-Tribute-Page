Daisuke's Timeline

  • 1973 - Born in Johannesburg, South Africa. Raised in Japan

  • 1997 - Joined SNK and began working on "The Last Blade"
  • 1998 - Created Guilty Gear, also voiced Sol Badguy
  • 2002 - Contributed to and helped deploy Guilt Gear XX
  • 2008 - Contributed heavily to Guilty Gear Accent Core Plus R
  • 2014 - Contributed to Guilty Gear Xrd
  • 2021 - Contributed to Guilty Gear Strive's soundtrack heavily
